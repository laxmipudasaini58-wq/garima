Garlic Chicken & Vegetable Stir-Fry



A quick and healthy dinner that is high in protein, rich in vitamins, and ready in about 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 200 g boneless chicken breast, sliced
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken and cook for 6 minutes until lightly browned.
  4. Add bell pepper, carrot, and broccoli.
  5. Stir-fry for 7 minutes until vegetables are tender but slightly crisp.
  6. Serve on its own or with rice or noodles.

Nutrition Highlights

Nutrient Benefit
Protein Supports muscle growth and repair
Fiber Helps digestion
Vitamins Supports overall health
Calories Moderate calorie meal

Vegetarian Option

Replace chicken with tofu, paneer, or chickpeas.
